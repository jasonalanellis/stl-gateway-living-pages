Preparing for Deep Cleaning

Before the Cleaning Team Arrives

Do:
- Pick up clutter and personal items
- Clear countertops as much as possible
- Communicate priority areas and concerns
- Note any fragile or special-care items
- Ensure access to all areas needing attention
- Secure pets if they might interfere

Consider:
- Moving small items you'd like cleaned behind/under
- Providing access codes or keys if not present
- Leaving specific product requests if you have preferences
- Noting any areas to avoid

Communication Tips

  • Discuss expectations clearly during booking
  • Point out any problem areas or concerns
  • Note surfaces requiring special care
  • Mention any products you prefer or want avoided
  • Provide feedback after the service

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deep cleaning take?

For an average Clayton home, expect 4-8 hours depending on size and condition. Larger homes or homes that haven't been deep cleaned recently may take longer.

Should I be home during deep cleaning?

It's not necessary. Many Clayton residents provide entry access and review results afterward. If you prefer to be home, that works too.

How often should I have deep cleaning?

Most homes benefit from deep cleaning 2-4 times per year, supplemented by regular maintenance cleaning. Homes with more traffic, children, or pets may benefit from more frequent deep cleaning.

Can deep cleaning remove all stains and buildup?

Professional deep cleaning dramatically improves most issues. However, some stains may be permanent or require specialized restoration. Honest cleaners will set realistic expectations.

What's the difference between deep cleaning and regular cleaning?

Regular cleaning maintains surface cleanliness. Deep cleaning addresses accumulated grime, detailed areas, and thorough attention to spots that regular cleaning doesn't reach.
