What to Expect from House Cleaning Services in St. Louis

Typical Pricing

House cleaning services in St. Louis typically charge based on the size of your home and the type of cleaning needed:

  • Standard cleaning: Generally ranges based on square footage and frequency
  • Deep cleaning: Typically 1.5 to 2 times the cost of standard cleaning
  • Move-out cleaning: Often priced per project based on home size and condition

Private cleaners may charge 20-50% less than professional services but often lack insurance and liability coverage.

What's Included in a Standard Cleaning

Most St. Louis house cleaning services include:
- Dusting all accessible surfaces
- Vacuuming and mopping floors
- Cleaning and sanitizing bathrooms
- Kitchen cleaning including countertops and appliances
- Making beds and tidying rooms
- Emptying trash

Deep cleaning adds tasks like:
- Inside oven and refrigerator cleaning
- Baseboards and light fixtures
- Window sills and blinds
- Cabinet fronts and door frames

Local Considerations for St. Louis Homeowners

Climate Factors

St. Louis experiences significant seasonal changes that affect home cleaning needs:

Summer: High humidity can lead to mold and mildew growth, particularly in bathrooms and basements. Regular cleaning helps prevent these issues from taking hold.

Winter: Salt and sand tracked in from treated roads and sidewalks can damage flooring. Many local cleaning services offer special attention to entryways during winter months.

Spring: Pollen counts in Missouri can be significant, making regular dusting and air quality maintenance important for allergy sufferers.

Neighborhood Considerations

Different St. Louis neighborhoods present unique cleaning challenges:

  • Historic districts like Soulard and Lafayette Square often have original hardwood floors, high ceilings, and detailed millwork requiring specialized care
  • Suburban areas like Kirkwood and Webster Groves typically have larger homes requiring more time and comprehensive service
  • Downtown and Central West End condos often need attention to modern finishes and shared building considerations

Tips for Hiring a House Cleaning Service

  1. Verify insurance and bonding - Protect yourself from liability if accidents occur
  2. Ask about their cleaning products - Important if you have allergies, pets, or children
  3. Request references - Reputable companies will gladly provide them
  4. Start with a deep clean - This establishes a baseline for regular maintenance
  5. Communicate your priorities - Let them know which areas matter most to you
  6. Discuss access - Determine if you'll provide a key or code for when you're not home

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my house professionally cleaned?

Most St. Louis families benefit from bi-weekly cleaning, though this varies based on household size, pets, and personal preferences. Homes with young children or multiple pets may benefit from weekly service.

Should I tip my house cleaner?

Tipping is appreciated but not required. If you're satisfied with the service, a tip of 15-20% or $10-20 per cleaning is customary.

What should I do before the cleaners arrive?

Pick up clutter and personal items so cleaners can focus on actual cleaning. Put away valuables and ensure pets are secured if they might interfere with the cleaning process.
